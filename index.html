About

About Godfrey “Alindi” Alindi

I’m a telecom and technology professional who enjoys making complex things work in the real world. Over the past decade, I’ve worked across the African continent on projects at the intersection of network deployment, project management and emerging technologies.

From greenfield rollout leadership to health and safety to AI-driven pilots, my work has always been about building systems that serve people at scale. A defining chapter of my career was helping build a nationwide network from zero, under immense pressure and with the responsibility of delivering safely.

Today, I’m deeply interested in the convergence of AI, telecom, sustainability and human-centred design. This site is where I document my trajectory, my projects and the evolution of my life’s work.
